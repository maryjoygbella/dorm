What is DormMatch?

DormMatch is dedicated to providing students with a convenient and stress-free way to find and book dormitories. Our platform simplifies the roommate selection process with a waitlisting feature, ensuring that students can focus on their studies while we take care of their housing needs.

Our Vision

To be the leading platform in student accommodation, ensuring that every student finds a home away from home with ease and convenience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the dormitory booking process, provide a wide range of options to meet different needs, and create a community where students feel safe, supported, and at home.

Our Team

We are a dedicated team of students with a passion for helping our co-students find the perfect living arrangements. Our team works tirelessly to ensure that the DormMatch platform is easy to use, reliable, and meets the highest standards of quality and service.
